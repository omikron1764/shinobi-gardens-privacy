Privacy Policy for Shinobi Gardens

Last updated: March 1, 2026

Compiler Error Studios LLC ("we," "us," or "our") operates the Shinobi Gardens mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in connection with your use of the App.

Information We Collect

Shinobi Gardens does not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information. The App stores game progress data locally on your device, including currency balances, owned units, farming progress, and campaign completion. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

Third Party Services

The App does not currently integrate any third party analytics, advertising, or data collection services.

Children's Privacy

Shinobi Gardens is appropriate for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13.
